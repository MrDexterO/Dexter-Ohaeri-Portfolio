About Dexter

Hi, I’m Dexter — a media professional with over two decades of experience in broadcasting and digital storytelling, including leading the award-winning > Channels' Visual Reality Interface .

I’m a co-recipient of the BEA Award for transmedia storytelling. Alongside my media work, I teach web and software product development, helping individuals and businesses build meaningful digital solutions.

My portfolio spans commercials, documentaries, and branded content, collaborating with creatives and global brands. My approach blends cinematic pacing, emotional depth, and precision.

Beyond media, I actively mentor aspiring developers—guiding them in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Python while helping them transition into real-world tech careers.

I also lead a nonprofit bootcamp focused on empowering young people, where outstanding participants receive funding to launch and scale their own ventures.
